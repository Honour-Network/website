There is a limited supply of asset-backed tokens with intrinsic value paying dividends. When the coin market nose dive, everyone loses tremendous value.

Acquiring fractional ownership of quality real world assets with cryptocurrency from anywhere in the world remains challenging.

Without seamless & borderless means to acquire assets, many have to pay high cost to invest their wealth paying inter- mediaries fat fees for limited value.
