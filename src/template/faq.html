Honour Network

Q: What is Honour Network FundTech?

Honour Network FundTech platform enables the creation of legitimate real world asset-backed tokens, empowers collective wisdom investing amongst the blockchain community where incentives are aligned and ensure transparent management of assets.
The FundTech platform is a decentralised blockchain application designed to adhere to the key principles of blockchain community and regulatory standards. We serve the blockchain community and regulated asset/fund managers.
Problems We Are Addressing:
- Lack of real world asset-backed tokens
- No collaboration on investing
- Incentives& risks are not aligned
- Centralised & closed approach to management of assets.

Q: How does Honour Network FundTech platform help?

Honour Network FundTech platform addresses the problems by:
- A pool of qualified investors with blockchain identity for asset managers
- Enable asset-backed tokens issue by licensed & regulated asset managers
- Investors buy asset-backed tokens directly without intermediaries
- Enable collective wisdom investing between asset managers and their investors
- Empower asset-backed token holders on investment & divestment
- Create the life-cycle of asset management on the blockchain
- Ensure on- and off-the-chain transparency & provenance using blockchain as single source of truth
- Enable efficient distribution of income & residue value on the blockchain
- Adhere To Regulatory Standards.

Q: What are the benefits of the Honour Network FundTech platform?

Benefits To Blockchain Community:
- Gain access to quality real world asset-backed tokens
- Gain transparency, accountability & open audit of their investment
- Gain the ability to vote on the investment & divestment
- Asset managers are motivated to collaborate

Benefits To Asset Managers:
- Existing pool of qualified investors
- Faster fundraising without intermediaries
- Access to a new source of funding - crypto community & crypto holders
- Ability to offer transparency, accountability & public audit efficiently with blockchain
- Attract a new generation of global customers who prefer transparency, immutability and openness of blockchain - “The Millennials”
- Lower cost of asset management & administration, increased efficiency on the blockchain - Ability to bring real word assets to the blockchain community.

Q: What else does Honour Network do?

We onboard customers & users with our wider community & industry development programs where operate & run innovation labs, support an ecosystem of solution partners and organize hosted conferences.
Finally, our business model includes a Social Give Back Programme with the objective of benefitting the socially excluded, not just financially excluded. It is our hope to set an example for future projects to include social give back as a standard deliverable in their business model.

Q: In summary, how does Honour Network FundTech platform work?

Investors Registration & Onboarding
- HN runs campaigns to sign-up users completing KYC/AML/CTF. These are potential qualified investors of AM
- HN sought “in-demand” investment opportunities from community using blockchain (1)
- Reward HNA to users who responded (2)
Fund Registration & Issue Asset-Backed Tokens
- AM deposits HNA on FundTech (2)
- AM adopts feedbacks from community (1)
- AM registers fund & issue asset-backed tokens
Fundraising From Crypto
- AM taps onto HN users pool& community for investors
- AM offers special payout & incentives for users who provided feedbacks (1)
- AM accepts Dai/ETH as subscription to fund (3)
Asset/Fund Management On Blockchain
- AM manages the full life-cycle of fund & asset-back tokens on FundTech/Blockchain (4)
- Investors can vote on fund investment & divestment (1)
- Investors can inspect transactions, documents &authorization on the blockchain & IPFS (4)
- AM deposit additional HNA monthly based on value of AUM (2)
- AM distribute income in Dai/ETH to asset-backed tokens holders (3)
- AM close the fund by destroying asset-backed tokens & distribute residual capital in Dai/ETH to asset-backed tokens holders (3)
Legend: HN - Honour Network AM - Asset Manager
(1) Collective Wisdom Investing (2) Interest Aligned
(3) Lower Cost, Better Efficiency (4) Transparent Investing.

Q: How is Honour Network different from others?

FundTech platform is not a fundraising or tokenization of assets platform which many projects offer. We different from others in the following ways:
1. We created the entire life-cycle of asset management on the blockchain to enable efficiency, lower cost & transparency.
2. We enable the open verification & audit of transactions, documents & authorisations made on the assets.
3. FundTech platform is a decentralised application.
4. We enable collaboration between the asset managers, amongst the blockchain community on investment & divestment decision.
5. We adhere regulatory standards required by the asset-backed token issuers.

The Company

Q: What is Honour Network?

Honour Network’s FundTech platform is a decentralised application on the blockchain that serves regulated& licensed asset managers allowing them to issue & manage asset-backed tokens on the blockchain for the asset-backed fund administered by them. Each asset-backed token is unique to the asset-backed fund, created as cryptographic tokens on the blockchain representing irrefutable ownership of the asset-backed fund.

Q: Why is the name of the company Honour Network?

The name reflects our commitment to enabling everyone in the world, regardless of their location, to be rewarded for their participation in the decentralized democratized fund raising economy.

Q: How long has Honour Network existed?

Honour Network is a new company, but the Honour Network technology based on Ethereum has been in development for many years within the community as a platform.

Honour Network has 10 employees and 6 advisors year to date and we are continue to expand our workforce.
