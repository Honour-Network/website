Life-Cycle Of Fund Creation And Management On The Blockchain

  • Investor Registration & Verification

    • Registration And Verification Of Investors Adhering To Regulatory Standards

  • Fund Token Creation & Sales

    • Creation of blockchain tokens representing the ownership of the Fund
    • Enable sales process of the Fund Tokens to investors

  • Fund Management

    • Management of Fund’s operations on the blockchain such as deployment of fund, distribution of income & residue value of the Fund.

  • Vote Management

    • Creation and management of voting mechanism
