Life-Cycle Of Fund Creation And Management On The Blockchain

  • Investor Registration & Verification

    • Registration And Verification Of Investors Adhering To Regulatory Standards

  • Fund Token Creation & Sales

    • Creation of blockchain tokens representing the ownership of the Fund
    • Enable sales process of the Fund Tokens to investors

  • Fund Management

    • Management of Fund’s operations on the blockchain such as deployment of fund, distribution of income & residue value of the Fund.

  • Vote Management

    • Creation and management of voting mechanism by the Funds’ token holders

Innovative Features Available On Honour Network FundTech Platform

  • Decentralized, Transparent, Accountable, With Blockchain As Single Source Of Truth & No Single Authority

  • On- And Off-The-Chain Transparency & Provenance Ensured

    • Everything Open, Public Verifiable & Auditable

  • Issue Quality & Asset-Backed Cryptocurrencies

  • Unique Blockchain Identity Adhering Regulatory Standards

  • Irrefutable Rights Of Token Holders On The Fund

  • Lower The Cost of Fund Management

    • Raise Fund Directly From Investors Efficiently
    • Distribute Fund Income & Residue Value To Token Holders Efficiently
